What Is Vyuha 2022 ?

The student association of the electronics and communication department (ESCA) with immense gratitude launching the department fest VYUHA, focusing on providing a platform for our emerging knowledge workers to bring out intrinsic motivation to showcase their aptitudes and associate with the tribal community. The 3 days-long department fest is dedicated to the theme of, ' CONNECTING TECH WITH NATURE' and will be held in the alluring campus versatility of GECW. We are directed on aspiring a huge number of students to spread knowledge, facilitate career opportunities, and instill awareness to educate the rural community.
